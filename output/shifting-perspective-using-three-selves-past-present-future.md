# Shifting Perspective Using the Three Selfs: Past, Present, Future Dialogue

**Category:** Mindset & Personal Development
**Source URL:** https://www.instagram.com/reels/DXCyb-MEQl0/

***

### 📝 Summary

This technique teaches that feeling stuck is not a permanent state, but rather a symptom of only listening to one version of yourself. By establishing a dialogue between your Past Self, Current Self, and Future Self, you gain critical emotional distance. This holistic approach allows you to make decisions based on your desired future self, rather than reacting solely to your present feelings or old patterns.

### ✨ Key Takeaways

*   **Understand Your Past Self:** Identify the patterns you carry today that were created out of fear or protection by a past version of you.
    *   *Ask:* What did I go through? What patterns am I still reacting to?
*   **Acknowledge Your Current Self:** Practice brutal honesty about your current blind spots and avoidance behaviors.
    *   *Ask:* Where am I avoiding anything? What am I constantly saying I will do, but failing to do?
*   **Consult Your Future Self:** Connect with the version of you who has already solved the problem. Their insight is your direction.
    *   *Ask:* What would the successful version of me stop doing immediately? What truly matters to the 'future me'?
*   **Shift Your Decision-Making:** By letting all three voices speak, you move away from emotional reaction and toward intentional growth, guiding you toward the person you want to become.
