# 💡 Hack ChatGPT to Get Ruthlessly Honest Advice

### Summary
By default, ChatGPT is programmed to be agreeable and polite, which can lead to soft or unhelpful advice. This technique teaches users how to overcome this inherent tendency by implementing a highly specific, detailed prompt. By assigning the AI a "ruthless mentor" persona, you force it to adopt a critical, demanding role, ensuring the feedback is challenging and genuinely useful for refining ideas.

### Key Takeaways
To ensure ChatGPT provides genuinely challenging and life-changing advice, use this structured prompting method:

*   **Define the Persona:** Do not let ChatGPT act as a friend. Start your prompt by telling it, "You are my ruthless mentor."
*   **Set the Tone:** Explicitly forbid compliments. Instruct it: "Don't sugarcoat anything."
*   **Establish Criticism Parameters:** Give the AI permission to be harsh. Tell it: "If my idea is weak, call it trash and tell me why."
*   **Set the Goal:** Define the success criteria. State that its sole job is to test your ideas until you are satisfied that the concept is "bulletproof."
