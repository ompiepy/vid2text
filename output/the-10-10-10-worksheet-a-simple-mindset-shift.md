### Summary

The 10-10-10 Worksheet is a quick, simple journaling exercise designed to instantly improve your mental state by activating three different areas of focus. By journaling about your desires, your current gratitude, and your favorite actions, you engage your mind across aspiration, appreciation, and action—a powerful trifecta that shifts you out of a negative or stuck state.

### Key Takeaways

Use a piece of paper and dedicate three sections to write down 10 items in each, focusing on the following components:

*   **✨ Desires (The Future):** List 10 things you truly desire. Do not limit yourself to what is realistic; focus on what genuinely excites you (e.g., traveling to a specific place, owning a car). This wakes up your sense of aspiration.
*   **🙏 Gratitude (The Present):** List 10 things you are genuinely grateful for. Keep these simple—basic human abilities (like the ability to breathe) or relationships (family). Focusing on what you already *have* instantly improves your perspective.
*   **🤸 Actions (The Joy):** List 10 things that you genuinely love to do. These should be activities that make you feel alive, such as hiking, spending time with friends, or going to the gym.

**💡 Why This Works:**
By completing all three sections, you hit three critical mental levels at once: what you **want** (future), what you **have** (present), and what you **can do** (action). This holistic approach pulls your state out of a bad mindset very quickly.
